Salesforce CPQ Maintenance & Workflow

Salesforce CPQ had been implemented by another team, but the quoting, contract, and renewal workflows required ongoing maintenance and integration improvements. I worked to ensure the system ran smoothly, incorporating Conga Contracts for approvals and automating renewals, while optimizing templates and workflows for the sales team.

Role
Salesforce CPQ Maintenance & Workflow Lead
Timeline
Ongoing
Platform
Salesforce CPQ, Conga
Deliverables
Workflow Mapping, Templates, Integration, Training

The Challenge

Although CPQ was already in place, multi-year contracts and approval workflows were inconsistent, manual touchpoints remained, and renewals weren’t automated. Sales teams needed predictable, reliable workflows to reduce errors and accelerate quoting.

Placeholder Image / Existing CPQ Workflow

My Approach

I mapped the existing quote-to-cash workflows to identify inefficiencies and inconsistencies. I maintained and optimized Salesforce CPQ, integrated Conga Contracts for approvals, and configured Salesforce Contracts to automate renewals.

I also optimized quote templates, documented workflows, and trained the global sales team to ensure adoption and smooth execution.

Placeholder Image / CPQ Workflow Mapping

The Solution

  • Maintained Salesforce CPQ workflows and templates
  • Integrated Conga Contracts for approval and generation
  • Automated renewal processes using Salesforce Contracts
  • Documented workflows and trained the global sales team
  • Improved efficiency and reduced errors across quoting and contracting
Placeholder Image / CPQ & Conga Integration

The Outcome

Quote generation became more predictable, approvals were automated through Conga Contracts, and renewals were streamlined. Sales teams had confidence in the system, pricing errors dropped, and workflow documentation improved adoption.
