Senior UX Designer – Long-term UX Owner

Marketing Campaign Tracking System

Over my tenure, I led the end-to-end UX for the company’s marketing and sales reporting platform, designing a centralized system that unified marketing campaigns, pipeline visibility, and lifecycle metrics. By connecting Salesforce Account Engagement, Sales Engagement, campaigns, and dashboards, I provided a single source of truth for marketing and sales teams, enabling data-driven decisions across campaigns, pipeline, MQLs, SQLs, and customer lifecycle stages.

Role
Lead UX Designer & Long-term UX Owner
Timeline
8+ Years
Platform
Salesforce Account Engagement & Sales Engagement
Deliverables
Research, UX, System Integration, Dashboards

The Problem

Data was siloed across HubSpot and Salesforce, making it difficult to understand campaign ROI, sales pipeline contributions, and the full customer lifecycle. Teams lacked a unified view, which slowed decision-making, limited visibility into MQLs, SQLs, and pipeline performance, and prevented accurate reporting.

Placeholder Image / Problem Diagram

My Approach

I mapped existing marketing and sales workflows, identified gaps, and conducted interviews with marketing, sales operations, and executives. Stakeholders needed a unified, actionable view where campaign performance, pipeline, and lifecycle metrics were accessible in real time.

  • Integrated Salesforce Account Engagement and Sales Engagement with existing CRM data
  • Designed a unified data model for campaign attribution and pipeline visibility
  • Built real-time dashboards to visualize ROI, pipeline, MQLs, SQLs, and customer lifecycle metrics
  • Connected Salesforce campaigns to manage campaign members and automate reporting
Placeholder Image / Workflow Diagram

I ensured that visualizations and dashboards were actionable, scalable, and aligned with business objectives. Regular feedback sessions and iterative improvements refined the system for both marketing and sales stakeholders.

The Solution

The outcome was a Salesforce-native, unified marketing and sales reporting platform. Users gained complete visibility into campaign performance, pipeline metrics, and lifecycle stages from a single dashboard.

  • Centralized database integrating Account Engagement, Sales Engagement, and Salesforce campaigns
  • Automated dashboards showing ROI, pipeline, MQLs, SQLs, and lifecycle metrics
  • Standardized reporting for stakeholders across marketing and sales
  • Real-time insights enabling data-driven decision-making
Placeholder Image / Final Dashboard Screens

The Outcome

Achieved end-to-end visibility into marketing contribution, pipeline performance, MQLs, SQLs, and customer lifecycle metrics. Automated reporting reduced manual effort and errors, allowing teams to focus on strategy and execution.

Marketing and sales leaders could now make informed, data-driven decisions about campaigns, budgets, and sales strategy—all from a single platform.
